About Me

Drone-based naturalistic driving research for safer autonomous vehicles.

I'm a PhD candidate in Vehicle Engineering at the School of Automotive Engineering, Jilin University (吉林大学), affiliated with the Automated Driving Safety Joint Lab and DriveResearch.Tech (驭研科技).

My research focuses on building naturalistic driving datasets from drone bird's-eye-view imagery — extracting vehicle and pedestrian trajectories, constructing HD maps, and mining safety-critical scenarios to advance autonomous driving safety evaluation.

Previously, I interned at Shenzhen Zhuoyu Technology as a functional safety engineer, where I developed the full aerial trajectory acquisition and analysis pipeline used in port autonomy and open-road AV testing.
